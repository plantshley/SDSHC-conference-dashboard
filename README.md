# SD Soil Health Coalition Conference Dashboard

An interactive data visualization dashboard analyzing survey results from the South Dakota Soil Health Coalition's Annual Soil Health Conferences (2022-2025).

## Overview

This dashboard presents comprehensive analysis of 319 conference survey responses across 4 years, demonstrating the Coalition's impact and reach across South Dakota's agricultural community.

## Key Findings

- **97% Implementation Rate**: 278 of 287 applicable respondents are "Likely" or "Very Likely" to implement changes - extraordinarily high impact
- **91% High Satisfaction**: 291 of 319 survey respondents rated the conference 4-5 stars, sustained across all years
- **56% Attendance Growth**: From 251 (2022) to 391 (2025) ticket registrations - total 1,307+ attendees over 4 years
- **International Reach**: Attendees from 260+ counties, 48 states & provinces, and 14 countries (Morocco, Greece, India, Fiji)
- **85% High Knowledge Gain**: 270 of 319 reported knowledge gain of 3-4 out of 4
- **Technician Program Concern**: Interest declined from 47% (2022) to 26% (2025) - needs investigation
- **2025 Student Surge**: Students & Educators jumped to 17.4% (from 4.6% in 2024)

## Notable Insights

### Topic Evolution (from PDF analysis)
- **Livestock Integration** #1 overall (17.2%) - peaked at 25% (2022), stabilized at 13% (2025)
- **Cover Crops** steady #2 (13.5%) - consistent 11-17% interest across years
- **Soil Biology Surging** - jumped from 4.6% (2024) to 15.9% (2025)
- **Regenerative Ag Trending Up** - increased from 0% (2022) to 13% (2025), statistically significant (p=0.005)
- **Economics** (8.7%) - professionals prioritize this (12-17%), producers focus on field practices
- **Topic Diversification** - "Other Topics" grew from 12% (2022) to 39% (2024), suggesting need for varied content

### Attendee Engagement
- Agricultural Producers show highest implementation likelihood
- Students & Educators demonstrate strong pipeline development
- Conservation Professionals maintain consistent participation
- Diverse reach: tribal land managers to commercial operations

## Usage

### Running Locally

1. Ensure all files are in the same directory
2. Open `dashboard.html` in a modern web browser
3. Navigate between sections using the top navigation tabs

### Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- JavaScript enabled
- Internet connection (for Chart.js CDN)
